Mama's Meatloaf

Description

My Mama's classic and easy meatloaf that I have loved since I was a child.

Ingredients

  • 2lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Pack Meatloaf Seasoning
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup Italian Breadcrumbs
  • 1 White Onion (diced)
  • 1 Bottle of Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. In a large bowl combine meat, seasoning, eggs, milk, breadcrumbs, and onion.
  3. Add meat mixture to a square baking dish.
  4. Press down to fill entire dish and form a flat surface on top.
  5. Generously coat top of meat mixture with ketchup.
  6. Bake until internal temperature reaches 160°F. About 60 minutes.
  7. Let the meatloaf cool for 10 to 15 minutes before slicing.
  8. Cut into preferred size portions and serve.
